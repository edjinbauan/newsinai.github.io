FEES AND PAYMENTS


All inquiries regarding tuition and other fees may be directed to the Accounting Office.

Payment Schemes
Students have the option to choose from two payment schemes:
• Full Payment
• Installment scheme (with installment interest charge of 10% for 10months or 1% per month), initial payment required = P5,000.00

If the student has opted to pay by installment and:
• His check payment bounces, the full amount becomes immediately due and demandable and must be paid in cash. Also, future check payments will no longer be accepted.

He fails to pay on due dates, a 1% penalty for late payment will be charged for every month of delay.

Upon enrolment, the student and/or his parent(s)/guardian(s) bind themselves to pay corresponding tuition and other school fees. In case of failure to pay off the due and demandable tuition and other school fees as scheduled,
or until the closing of the semester/Quarter, it is understood that the students and parent(s)/guardian(s) recognized, without reservation, the night/authority of the school to:
• Suspend the students from taking periodical exams;
• Withhold the records of the students; or
• Pay the penalty clause, if any, without need of judicial intervention or permission.

Mode of Payments
Payment may be made only in cash or personal check.

• It is presumed that the personal check payments are backed with sufficient funds. If a check bounces, it must be replaced immediately with cash plus Php 1,000.00 penalty. Future check payments will no longer be accepted.
Students are considered enrolled only after payment of the required fees

3. Reimbursement.
If student has paid the pertinent tuition and other fees in full or installment but voluntary and in writing withdraws from school,
transfers to another school, or drop in the beginning of classes, he shall be entitled to a refund of tuition and/or other fees based on the following schedule:

For Regular Semester/Quarters:
Within the first week of classes
Within the second week
After the second week 		75% Refund
50% Refund
No Refund

This schedule shall be followed whether or not the student attended classes. Refund will be processed only after the student has obtained the necessary clearance.

Refunds due to overpayment, or overpayment resulting from a change in the student’s academic load, are credited toward subsequent enrolments.
However may be reimbursed upon submission of a letter from the student’s parent(s)/guardian(s).

4. Arrears
If the student has unsettled accounts or arrears from the previous semester/year, he may enroll for the following semester only after these accounts or arrears plus the corresponding finance charges have been settled.
