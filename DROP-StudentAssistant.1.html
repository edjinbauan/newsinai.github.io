STUDENT ASSISTANTSHIP PROGRAM



Student Assistantship Program
• This is a financial assistance program to deserving students who wish to earn extra income to supplement the financial requirement of their program.
• Students who avail of the Students Assistantship Program shall be classified as working students and not as regular employee of the school.
• Student Assistants shall pass all the necessary requirements given by the school administration.


spacing
Requirements:
• Applicant must have at least one (1) semester of study at New Sinai School and Colleges.
• Letter of Application addressed to the School Administrator.
• Letter of Consent for parents.
• Work Schedule in the office assignment.
• Latest Income Tax Return or Certificate of Tax Exemption.
• Grades of previous semester (General Average of 87% with no grades lower than 85 in all learning areas/subjects).
• Registration form with schedule of classes for the current semester.
• One piece of 1x1 and 2x2 photo.
• Application Form.
• Student Assistant Contract.
