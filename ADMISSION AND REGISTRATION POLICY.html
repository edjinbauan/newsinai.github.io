ADMISSION AND REGISTRATION POLICY


ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS
To be eligible for admission to preschool, Elementary and High School, the student who wish to enrol must present the following credentials

Preschool
The age requirements for Nursery must be three to four (3-4) year old;
Kinder 1 must be four to five (4-5) year old; Kinder 2 must be five to six (5-6) year old and may be accepted upon the presentation of the PSA Birth Certificate,
Four (4) copies of 2x2 colored picture if passed the initial interview by the Admission committee.
Grade School

NEW ENROLEES TRANSFEREES

    Enrolment Procedure
    Enrolment is the official act of the school in accepting students, provided that they complied with the payments of the required fees and the prescribed rules and regulations for admission.

    Registration
    Students must enrol on the dates designated for the respective year and course. Every student is required to follow the enrolment procedures according to the instruction posted at the Bulletin Board during enrolment period.

    Orientation
    New Enrolees and Transferees are required to attend orientation program initiated by the Guidance Office on a date indicated.
