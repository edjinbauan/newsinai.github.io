HISTORY OF THE NEW SINAI SCHOOL AND COLLEGES STA. ROSA INC.


(Formerly New Sinai Educational System of Sta. Rosa, Inc.)
In 2004, the new core members of New Sinai MDI Hospital convened to reorganize MDI (then a diagnostic clinic in Muntinlupa) with an increase capitalization forwarded to SEC the following three-phase plan:
  • To put up tertiary hospital
  • To put up a School
  • To build a commercial apartelle

    • The New Sinai MDI Hospital, a tertiary hospital opened on July 7, 2007 in the newly-built six-storey building in Tagapo, Sta. Rosa Laguna. Organizational meeting for the school started in October 2007 in Maxâ€™s Tunasan, Muntinlupa.
    The President of NSMDIH, Rosalinda P. Deala MD, convened the 26 core members of MDI for the purpose of putting up a school. The meeting was conducted in the presence of Atty. LindaJimeno as legal counsel.
    Several options were discussed but the final decision approved by the majority was to put up new corporation- thus the New Sinai Educational System of Sta. Rosa, Laguna, Inc., was born on June 20, 2008.
    This was later on changed to NEW SINAI SCHOOL AND COLLEGES STA. ROSA, INC., on February 23, 2011.
    SEC papers, constitution and by-laws were prepared for submission through its interim chairman, Nelia A. Barlis, Ed.D. SEC approval was obtained on December 14, 2007.
